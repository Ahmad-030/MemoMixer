OVERVIEW

About This Policy

MemoMixer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

🔒

Short version: Everything stays on your device. We never collect, upload, or share your personal data — ever.

💾
01 · DATA

Data We Collect

MemoMixer stores all your notes — including photos, audio recordings, captions, and tags — locally on your device.

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. Your notes belong to you, and only you.

🛡️
02 · PERMISSIONS

App Permissions

We only request permissions that are strictly necessary for core features:

📷 Camera Capture photos for your notes
🎙️ Microphone Record audio memos
🖼️ Photo Library Pick existing photos from gallery
🚫
03 · THIRD-PARTY

Third-Party Services

MemoMixer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

✅

No ads. No trackers. No data brokers. Zero.

🔐
04 · SECURITY

Data Security

All your notes are stored in your device's internal storage and SharedPreferences, protected by your device's own security model.

We recommend keeping your device secured with a PIN or biometric lock for an added layer of protection.

👶
05 · CHILDREN

Children's Privacy

MemoMixer is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

📝
06 · CHANGES

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
